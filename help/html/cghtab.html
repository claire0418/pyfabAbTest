CGH Tab

The CGH tab provides access to the calibration constants used in calcuating computer-generated holograms. Change values by entering numbers into the form and hitting RETURN.

Instrumental Calibrations

Wavelength
Vacuum wavelength of the trapping laser [μm]
Refractive index
Refractive index of the medium
Magnification
Magnification of the objective lens
Focal length
Focal length of the objective lens [μm]
Camera pitch
Center-to-center separation between camera pixels [μm]
SLM pitch
Center-to-center separation between SLM pixels [μm]
Scale factor
Effective magnification of the objective's input pupil onto the SLM

Alignment at the camera plane

(xc, yc)
Coordinates of the optical axis in the camera plane [pixel]
zc
Axial offset of the trap plane from the image plane [pixel]
θc
Orientation of the trap plane relative to the image plane [degree]

Alignment at the SLM plane

(xs, ys)
Coordinates of the optical axis in the hologram plane [pixel]
φs
Tilt of the SLM relative to the optical axis [degree]
k0
Splay factor [rad/pixel]
