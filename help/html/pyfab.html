Overview

pyfab is a video interface for holographic optical trapping and holographic video microscopy.

pyfab's controls are grouped into eight tabs in the bar above this browser. Navigate onto a tab by clicking on it. This Help Browser is located in the Help tab. What the tabs control is described in the links below.
