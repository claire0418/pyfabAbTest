How to Work with Traps

Create a trap
by holding the SHIFT key while right-clicking on the screen. A marker will appear on the screen (approximately) where the trap is projected. In addition, an entry for the new trap will appear in the trap tab.
Move a trap (or a group of traps)
by left-dragging the trap's marker. The selected trap's marker will turn red as it is being moved, and will turn green again when the mouse button is released. Alternatively, the trap's position can be changed with controls in the trap tab.
Move a trap in 3D
by placing the mouse over the trap's marker and scrolling the mouse wheel. The axial (z) position can be monitored and set precisely with the trap tab.
Delete a trap (or a group of traps)
by holding the CONTROL key while right-clicking on the trap's marker. The marker will disappear, and the trap's information will disappear from the trap tab.
Gather traps into a group
by left-dragging the mouse on the screen to create a "rubber-band" box around the traps. Markers in the group will turn yellow while being gathered, and return to green after the mouse button is released. After this, the entire group can be moved simultaneously by left-dragging or scrolling any of the traps in the group. Individual traps can still be moved within a group using the trap tab.
Remove traps from a group
by holding the CONTROL key while left-clicking the trap to be removed. This trap will now move independently of the rest of the group.
