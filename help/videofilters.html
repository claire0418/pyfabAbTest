Video Filters

A video filter performs an operation on each frame coming from the video camera and then passes along the transformed frame for further filtering or for display. Filters are applied sequentially in the order they are selected. Some "filters" may not change an image, but instead might extract information.

Select a filter by clicking on its check box

Filters

Median
Performs a running median filter on the image stream. Moving objects disappear and the stationary background remains.
Normalize
Divide (normalize) each frame with a running median. This emphasizes moving foreground features and suppresses the static background.
Sample and Hold
Estimate the background with a running median filter and then use that estimate to normalize subsequent frames.
