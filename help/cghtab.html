CGH Tab

The CGH tab provides access to the calibration constants used in calcuating computer-generated holograms. Change values by entering numbers into the form and hitting RETURN.
qpp
Overall scale factor [mrad/pixel]
alpha
Aspect ratio
(xc, yc)
Coordinates of optical axis in camera plane [pixel]
zc
Axial offset of trap plane from image plane [pixel]
theta
Orientation of trap plane relative to image plane [degree]
(xs, ys)
Coordinates of optical axis in hologram plane [pixel]
k0
Splay factor [1/pixel]
